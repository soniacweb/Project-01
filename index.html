Want to hear your story before playing?

In a galaxy far, far away..

You are Earth's last hope, Thor. You must defeat the Space Invaders trying to take over the galaxy. The aim of the game is to shoot the invading alien armada, before it reaches our beloved planet's surface using your hammer.

Make it rain lightning and you better be quick! The aliens don't come in peace and will periodically drop bombs towards you.

Peace will be restored to the galaxy only after you have sucessfully destroyed all the aliens- no pressure. The aim is to achieve the highest score possible before either being destroyed by the aliens, or allowing them to reach the planet's surface.

Good luck!
